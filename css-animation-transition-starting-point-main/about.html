About Mochas & Mimosas

“Empowered Sips. Unstoppable Vibes.”

Our Vision

To create a viral, vibrant, and inclusive cafe-lounge space that blends bold coffee culture with bubbly brunch energy — where every woman (and ally) feels celebrated, seen, and empowered.

Our Mission

To serve up handcrafted mochas, signature mimosas, and curated bites in a space designed for connection, creativity, and confidence — setting the standard for coffee and brunch culture in San Antonio.

Our Community

  • Women ages 25–45
  • Young professionals, entrepreneurs, and creatives
  • College students from UTSA, Trinity, UIW
  • Brunch-lovers, bachelorettes, and besties
  • LGBTQ+ inclusive and empowering

What We Value

  • Aesthetic, Instagram-worthy spaces
  • Community, confidence, and conversation
  • Trendy experiences and social connection
  • Empowerment and fun in every sip

Whether you're snapping brunch photos, closing a deal over coffee, or celebrating sisterhood — Mochas & Mimosas is your vibe hub in San Antonio.
